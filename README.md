**Project Overview :**

1) Business Insights 360 is an enterprise-level analytics dashboard developed for **AtliQ Hardware**, a global computer hardware manufacturer.

2) As AtliQ Hardware expanded into multiple international markets, business stakeholders found it increasingly difficult to monitor performance across different business functions using static reports.

3) This dashboard provides a centralized analytics platform that enables management to monitor key business metrics, identify trends, compare performance against benchmarks and targets, and make data-driven decisions across multiple departments.

**Problem Statement :**

AtliQ Hardware has recently expanded its operations into multiple global markets.

With increasing business complexity, leadership requires a centralized reporting solution that can provide insights into different functional areas including:

i) Finance
ii) Sales
iii) Marketing
iv) Supply Chain
v) Executive Performance

The objective of this project is to replace fragmented reporting with an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables users to monitor business performance, compare KPIs with benchmarks and targets, and drill into detailed insights across customers, products, regions, and time periods.

**Dataset Information :**

i) **Company:** AtliQ Hardware
ii) **Currency Used:** USD (Millions)
iii) **Actual Sales Data:** September 2017 – December 2021
iv) **Estimated Data:** January 2022 – August 2022
v) **Fiscal Year:** September → August (Next Calendar Year)
